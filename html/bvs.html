Facilities:

BVS (Bai Virbaiji Soparivala Parsi High School) is renowned for its comprehensive facilities, offering an environment conducive to both academic and extracurricular growth. The school features modern classrooms, science and computer labs, and specialized rooms for arts and music. BVS also offers sports facilities including sports fields, a swimming pool, and a gymnasium, ensuring that students have access to a wide range of physical and recreational activities.

BVS emphasizes a well-rounded approach to education, focusing on academic performance, discipline, and character development. The school follows policies that encourage students to be respectful, responsible, and proactive in their studies. Regular assessments are conducted to track academic progress, and feedback is provided to both students and parents to ensure continuous development. The school also offers opportunities for students to engage in various extracurricular activities, contributing to their personal growth.
