Information About The City School

Facilities:

The City School in Karachi is part of an international network that offers modern educational facilities designed to enhance student learning. The school provides well-equipped classrooms, science and computer labs, a library, and sports facilities. The City School is committed to providing an environment where students can pursue both academic excellence and personal development through a variety of extracurricular activities such as sports, music, and arts.

Policies:

The City School follows a selective admission process, ensuring that students who are admitted are well-suited for the school’s academic standards. The policies focus on holistic development, balancing academics with the development of character and life skills. The school values discipline, responsibility, and respect, while regularly monitoring students’ academic and social progress through assessments and parent-teacher interactions.

Eligibility:

Admissions are open to students from early years to A-Level, with the eligibility criteria depending on the grade level. Age requirements must be met for each class, and the admission process includes an entrance exam and interview. Students are selected based on their academic potential and readiness for the respective grade level.
