Information About The Smart School

Facilities:

Smart School in Karachi provides a cutting-edge, technology-driven learning environment. The classrooms are equipped with interactive smart boards, allowing for engaging and dynamic lessons. Students also have access to computer labs with modern technology, a library stocked with educational resources, and sports grounds for physical activities. The school emphasizes both academic learning and the development of students' technological skills, preparing them for the future.

Policies:

Smart School focuses on a child-centered approach to education, where individual learning needs are met through personalized instruction. The school follows a disciplined environment, stressing the importance of punctuality, behavior, and respect. Regular assessments are conducted to track students' progress and performance, and report cards are shared with parents. The school encourages active participation in extracurricular activities, providing opportunities for students to explore various interests beyond academics.

Eligibility:

Smart School offers education from pre-school through to secondary school levels. The admission process includes an entrance test and an interview, with age requirements that vary by grade. Each student’s academic performance, as well as their suitability for the class based on age, is assessed for admission.
